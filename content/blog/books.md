Are you looking for a new book to read? Here are a few of my favorites I have listened to or read.

## Conversion Stories
 - **Defying Jihad** by Esther Ahmad
 - **Seeking Allah, Finding Jesus** by Nabiel Quareshi
 - **Saving My Assassin** by Virginia Prodan

## Mission Stories
Stories from all over the world of missionaries going out in faith and God doing great things through them.
 - **God's Double Agent** by Bob Fu
 - **Peace Child** by Don Richardson
 - **I'm Not Leaving** by Carl Wilkens
 - **Evidence Not Seen** by Darlene Deibler Rose
 - **Hudson Taylor** by Janet & Geoff Benge
 - **Brother Andrew**

Thats all for now. I'll update the list as I read more books.
